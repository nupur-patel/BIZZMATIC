Seasonal Email Campaigns

We never miss an opportunity to have clients coming back and spending more. You bet every holiday and season change will send out customized email campaingns to each of your clients. New Years, Black Friday, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas... Fall, Winter, Spring, Summer... There's always an occasion for your business to take advantage of.

Birthday Promos

The goal is to ensure your clients feels special and Birthdays are the perfect way to do this. We'll handle this tedious task and make your clients feel spoiled by sending them personalized promos a week before their birthday. When the special day hits, they'll know to drop by a visit they wouldn't have made otherwise.

Post-Appointment Client Contact

It's no secret that up-keeping relationships with your clients is key in the beauty industry. However it's time consuming and unrealistic. We'll take care of this by sending your clients "thanks for visiting!" messages after their appointments. We'll also prompt them to book their next visit for an additional offer ensuring no procrastination occurs.

Automated Booking system

Putting an end to your customers booking appointments with you over text. No more going back and forth over the phone. We've automated your bookings as well. A simple link is given to your clients so they can self-book appointments around your availability
